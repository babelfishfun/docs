Sign Up Process

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Choose Your Method: Select Web3 wallet, social media, or email signup
  2. Complete Authentication: Follow the prompts for your chosen method
  3. Set Your Language: Choose from 100+ supported languages
  4. Create Your Profile: Add a display name and profile picture
  5. Connect Wallet (Optional): Link a Solana wallet for $BABEL tokens
  6. Start Exploring: Join communities and start communicating!

Language Selection

During signup, you'll be asked to select your primary language. This will be the language you see all translated content in. You can change this setting anytime in your profile.

Supported Languages

Babelfish supports 100+ languages including:

  • English, Spanish, French, German, Italian
  • Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean
  • Arabic, Hindi, Portuguese, Russian
  • And many more regional languages

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Wallet Connection Issues: Make sure you have a Solana wallet installed and are on the correct network
  • Email Verification: Check your spam folder for verification emails
  • Social Login Problems: Ensure your social media accounts are active and accessible
  • Language Not Available: Contact support if your preferred language isn't listed
