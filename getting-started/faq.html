Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Babelfish and getting started.

General Questions

What is Babelfish?

Web3 communication platform with AI-powered translation across 100+ languages, inspired by the Babel fish from The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy.

How does translation work?

Uses xAI Grok API for real-time translation of text, audio, and video with cultural context understanding.

Is it free?

Yes! Basic features are free including translation, communities (up to 200 members), and $BABEL token earning.

What's different?

Web3 integration, flexible identity system, community-focused features, and cross-platform availability.

Getting Started

How do I sign up?

Sign up with social media (X, Facebook, LinkedIn), email, or Web3 wallet. Visit babelfish.app to get started.

Do I need a Solana wallet?

No! We create one automatically if you sign up with social media or email. Connecting an existing wallet gives you full control over $BABEL tokens.

How do I set my language?

Select your primary language from 100+ options during registration. Change anytime in settings.

What if I don't speak English?

Babelfish supports 100+ languages with fully translated interface. Use the platform entirely in your native language.

$BABEL Token

What is $BABEL?

Utility token powering the Babelfish ecosystem. Built on Solana for governance, rewards, and premium features.

How do I earn $BABEL?

Earn through invitations (10 $BABEL per invite), community participation, staking, and platform usage.

Where can I buy $BABEL?

Available on Solana DEXes: Raydium (primary), Orca, Jupiter, and other Solana DEXes.

What can I do with $BABEL?

Vote in governance, access premium features, earn 8-12% APR through staking, and trade on DEXes.
